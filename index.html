Aine’s Homemade Hot Chocolate

Simple, warm, and perfect for a cozy day in ☕

What You'll Need

  • 2 cups of milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows for topping

How to Make It

  1. Pour the milk into a saucepan and warm it over medium heat.
  2. Whisk in the cocoa powder and sugar until fully dissolved.
  3. Let the mixture heat through without boiling, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the vanilla extract, if using, and stir once more.
  5. Serve hot in your favorite mug. Top with whipped cream or marshmallows, and enjoy!
